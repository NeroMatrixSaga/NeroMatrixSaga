MATRIX INFORMATION

The following data fragments were recovered after the collapse. Some records remain incomplete. Some truths are restricted.

WHAT IS THE MORTAL MATRIX?

The Mortal Matrix is not a machine, nor a singular entity. It is a system that emerged when reality fractured during the Tragedy of Geneva.

It governs how energy, power, and survival function within the altered world. Those who interact with it are altered in return.

WHY MODERN WEAPONS FAILED

Entities that crossed into our reality do not fully exist within conventional physical laws.

Ballistic weapons, explosives, and traditional technology proved ineffective. Only direct energy interactions showed measurable impact.

ENERGY-BASED INTERACTION

The Matrix enables the conversion of external energy into a form the human body can withstand.

This process is unstable. Improper synchronization often leads to cellular collapse, neural damage, or complete system rejection.

WHY HUMANITY SURVIVED

Survival was not achieved through strength, but through adaptation.

Those capable of synchronizing with the Matrix gained the ability to interact with entities from beyond the fractured dimensions.

INCOMPLETE RECORDS

Large portions of Matrix data remain missing or corrupted. Whether this is due to system instability or deliberate restriction is unknown.

Further fragments may unlock as the system stabilizes.
